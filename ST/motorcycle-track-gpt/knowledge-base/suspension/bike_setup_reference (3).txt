
Bike Setup and Relevant Information for Motorcycle Racing:

1. Bike Model: 
    - Year, make, model.
2. Weight:
    - Bike weight (without rider).
    - Rider weight with gear.

3. Suspension Settings:
    - Front suspension spring rate.
    - Rear suspension spring rate.
    - Front fork oil height.
    - Oil viscosity.
    - Front compression settings (clicks).
    - Front rebound settings (clicks).
    - Rear compression settings (clicks).
    - Rear rebound settings (clicks).
    
4. Tyre Information:
    - Front tyre size.
    - Rear tyre size.
    - Tyre brand and model (e.g., Pirelli Diablo Supercorsa).
    - Tyre pressures (front and rear).

5. Gear Ratio:
    - Front sprocket size (teeth).
    - Rear sprocket size (teeth).

6. Aerodynamics:
    - Any modifications affecting aerodynamics (winglets, fairing changes).

7. Engine Tuning:
    - Any engine tuning or modifications.
    
8. Environmental Conditions:
    - Air temperature.
    - Track temperature.

9. Track Information:
    - Track name.
    - Key features (length, types of turns, straight lengths).

This reference will serve to ensure the bike's setup is optimally configured for performance under specific racing conditions and environments.
